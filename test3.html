About Steph Curry

Stephen Curry is my all time favourite basketball player.

Stephen Curry is considered one of the most exciting and influential players in basketball history because of his unmatched shooting ability, creativity, and flair. His deep three-pointers, often taken from far beyond the arc, have completely changed how the game is played — encouraging teams to embrace faster, more perimeter-oriented offenses.

What makes Curry special isn't just his shooting accuracy, but also his quick release, incredible ball-handling, and off-the-ball movement, which constantly keeps defenders guessing. Fans love watching him because he plays with joy, confidence, and a sense of fearlessness, often turning games into highlight reels.

His relatively smaller size (by NBA standards) also makes him more relatable and inspiring — showing that skill, work ethic, and determination can outshine pure physical dominance.

Accolades

    3× NBA Champion (2015, 2017, 2018)

  • Curry led the Golden State Warriors to three championships, becoming a pivotal part of their dynasty.

    • 2× NBA MVP (2015, 2016)

  • He won back-to-back MVP awards, including the first-ever unanimous MVP in 2016, a testament to his dominance and influence on the game.

    • NBA All-Star Selections (8×)

  • Curry has been selected to the NBA All-Star game multiple times, showcasing his consistent excellence and recognition among his peers.

    • NBA All-Time Leader in 3-Pointers Made

  • Curry holds the record for the most three-pointers made in NBA history, surpassing Ray Allen in 2021, and solidifying his legacy as the greatest shooter ever.

    • NBA Scoring Champion (2021)

  • Center

    • Curry led the league in scoring during the 2020-2021 season, averaging 32.0 points per game at the age of 33, proving he’s still a dominant force even as his career progresses.
